# Review Plan

The review plan establishes crucial validation gates and required evidence checks for major governance, legal, sequencing, and operational decisions, focusing on the timing constraints imposed by the Builder's Blueprint strategy, particularly regarding the delayed opt-out repeal relative to ERM II convergence requirements. Key parameters subject to verification include KPI thresholds for societal readiness, the strict 24-month ERM II participation period, and the governance structure's ability to manage resource allocation and technical risk independently of political timelines. Failure to meet these dependency checks, such as securing EU agreement on the sequencing trade-off or maintaining fiscal discipline during the long convergence period, directly propagates timeline and funding risks throughout the entire project duration.
